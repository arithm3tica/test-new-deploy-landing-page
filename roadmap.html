Arithmetica Roadmap

2018

Feb 2018

PoC

The original Arithmetica PoC was developed in 36 hours at ETHDenver.

May 2018

High-level Design Document Complete

After experimenting with WASM, GPU.js and various work distribution methods, we were able to complete a high level design document.

Jun 2018

Landing Page

The new Arithmetica landing page is created and styled with the new logo and design elements.

Aug 2018

MVP

The MVP will feature a more flexible way to define new problems, visualize results and store the resulting data.

Nov 2018

Initial Release at DEVCON 4

The initial release will have support for WASM and add more features to encourage collaboration: ERC-721 Participation Badges, User Profiles, User Blog Posts.

© 2018 Arithmetica

Arithmetica represents a new form of off-chain scaling in the blockchain space by combining Ethereum, IPFS, libP2P, and Infura to utilize computational cycles donated by altruistic users. Distributed altruism is the hallmark feature of Arithmetica; it avoids the complications that can arise from incentivization, eliminates data silos, and enables computational collaboration.
